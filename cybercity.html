80x40 procedural cyberpunk cityscape:

  • Buildings: procedural heights via sin(x*0.3) + cos(x*0.17)
  • Neon: phase-shifted pulses sin(t*2 + phase + x*0.1)
  • Rain: gravity sim with wind drift sin(t*0.5)
  • Holograms: warp/fade via sin(t*freq + phase)
  • Traffic: velocity vectors along street level
  • Puddles: ripple with sin(t*4 + offset)
  • Glitch: random char flips, line shifts
